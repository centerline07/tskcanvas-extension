Privacy Policy

MasterCanvas Tab Saver Browser Extension
Last updated: December 31, 2025

Overview

MasterCanvas Tab Saver ("the Extension") is a browser extension that allows you to save your open browser tabs to your MasterCanvas account. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

When you use the Extension to save tabs, we collect the following information:

  • Tab URLs: The web addresses of the tabs you choose to save
  • Tab Titles: The titles of the web pages you choose to save
  • Favicon URLs: The icon URLs associated with your tabs (for display purposes)
  • Authentication Data: Your email address and account identifier for authentication purposes

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To save your selected tabs to your MasterCanvas account
  • To authenticate you and associate saved tabs with your account
  • To display your saved tabs within the MasterCanvas application

Data Storage

Your tab data is stored securely on our servers powered by Convex. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your authentication is handled securely through Clerk, a trusted authentication provider.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: We use Clerk for authentication and Convex for data storage. These services process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have stored about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Choose which tabs to save (the Extension allows you to select/deselect individual tabs)
  • Revoke access at any time by uninstalling the Extension

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • Tabs: To read the URLs and titles of your open tabs so you can save them
  • Storage: To store your preferences locally
  • Cookies: To maintain your authentication session with MasterCanvas

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
